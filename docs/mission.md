# Provisional Canvas

## Vision

A canvas for a world reinventing itself — provisional by design, so you're never stuck with yesterday's version of tomorrow.

## Mission

To bring Superhuman's flow state and Notion's blank-slate customizability to agent orchestration — a calm, focused workspace where you move between agents naturally, pick up conversations when they need you, and carry context across all of them without breaking flow. The canvas is provisional — no two creators are the same, and neither are their choice of tools. The canvas becomes yours the moment you shape it around how you think, and how your agents operate.
